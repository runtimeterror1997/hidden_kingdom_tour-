# Hidden Kingdom Tours - Website Owner's Guide

This guide explains how your website works in plain English. Think of your website as a digital extension of your office in Bhutan.

---

## 1. How Customers Contact You
When someone visits your site and wants to ask a question:

1.  **The Customer's Action**: They fill out the "Contact Us" form with their name and question.
2.  **The Digital Postman**: The website automatically bundles their message and sends it to you.
3.  **Your Inbox**: You receive an email at **oasistours.info@gmail.com**.
4.  **Replying**: You just hit "Reply" in your email, and the message goes directly back to the customer.

## 2. How Bookings Work
When a customer is ready to book their dream trip:

1.  **The Booking**: They fill out the formal "Book Now" form.
2.  **Special Handling**: Because bookings are important, these go to a separate specialized inbox.
3.  **Your Booking Inbox**: You receive the full details at **oasistours.booking@gmail.com**.
4.  **Next Step**: You can then start preparing their itinerary and permits immediately.

## 3. Managing Your "Traveler Stories"
You can change the testimonials (reviews) on your homepage without needing any technical skills.

1.  **The Dashboard**: You log into a simple website called **Contentful** (it works like a simplified Facebook or Word document).
2.  **Adding Stories**: You type in the traveler's name, their home country, and their kind words.
3.  **Magic Update**: As soon as you hit "Publish," the website automatically updates itself to show the new story to the world.
